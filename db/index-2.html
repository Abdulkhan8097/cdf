What we Did

Cosmo Projects

Rural Sanitation

Is another core focus area that you'll find on our home page. We recognize the significance of clean and hygienic living conditions, especially in rural areas. Our projects in this domain aim to improve sanitation facilities, promote proper waste management, and educate communities about hygiene practices, ultimately leading to healthier and more sustainable living environments.

Drinking Water

Is a basic necessity, yet millions still lack access to clean, safe drinking water. We are dedicated to addressing this pressing issue by implementing water purification projects and promoting sustainable water resource management practices.

Green Cosmo

Represents our dedication to environmental sustainability. Through various green initiatives, we aim to reduce our carbon footprint, protect natural ecosystems, and promote eco-friendly practices.

EDP

Empowers rural youth to build sustainable livelihoods locally, curbing urban migration. By harnessing local resources and nurturing entrepreneurial skills, we equip them to succeed while protecting the environment. "Best Volunteer" program, recognizing the invaluable contributions of dedicated individuals who volunteer their time and expertise to make a difference. We celebrate their commitment and showcase their stories on our home page as a source of inspiration for others.

Medical Help

We believe that access to quality healthcare is a fundamental right, and our foundation actively supports those in need by sponsoring critical medical treatments for the most vulnerable individuals. Through our initiatives, we aim to ensure that no one is left behind when it comes to receiving the medical care they deserve.

Fighting for Air

Signifies our commitment to combating air pollution and promoting clean air initiatives. We actively work towards reducing emissions, raising awareness about the importance of air quality, and advocating for policies that protect our environment and respiratory health.

Renewable Energy

Is a cornerstone of our mission to build a more sustainable future. We invest in renewable energy projects, such as solar and wind power, to reduce our reliance on fossil fuels and promote clean, green energy alternatives.
